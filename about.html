About Us

  • First portable monitor
  • First VGA LCD kit under $200
  • First TFT LCD kit under $1,000 (1994)
  • First 7.8″ passive LCD monitor

    • EarthLCD products and solutions are in medical devices, embedded systems, airplanes, and factories worldwide.     AMAZING third generation, intelligent, all-in-one, touchscreen "ezLCD" technology. Highly functional, flexible, cost-effective alternative to buttons, switches, character modules, STN displays or more complex graphic touch displays. Easy to program via USB development cable (sold separately) and your own PC! Simple ASCII command-driven operation using our free serial terminal program (included). Command set is intuitive and includes a multitude of fonts, images, and sample macros to get you going fast! Widgets include: analog meter, buttons, checkbox, dial, radio button, slider, and others! Works with any micro-controller, including Arduino!
